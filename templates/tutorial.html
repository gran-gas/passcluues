1. What is Logged?


Logged it's a anonymous web tool to create clues or reminders of the sites in wich you are logged


2. How to use Logged?


2.1 Open Logged index page and generate your access key





2.1.1 Why i can't create my own key?


the purpose of Logged is that you do not enter any direct personal information, a secure key is auto-generated for you, but it's not related to your name


2.1.2 Do i need to remember my key?


We recommend that you copy and paste it in a secure directory on your pc or smartphone


2.1.2 What if i want to delete my account?


There is no account, just delete (forget) your key


2.2 Login with your generated key


2.3 Enter a site where you want to add a remainder, a URL and a color for the site name



2.3.1 What kind of reminders?


PASSWORD CLUES: insert personal clues for your password in the site, NEVER INSERT THE ACTUAL PASSWORD!


WHY AM I LOGGED HERE?: usually we logged into sites for a particular reason that we later forget, insert that reason here



2.4 You can delete a site selecting delete site button in the main page
